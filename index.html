About

I am a PhD student major in bioinformatics in Zhejiang University - University of Edinburgh joint institute. I am currently working in labW led by Dr. Wanlu Liu. I am currently working on genomic/multi-omics algorithm development, analysis, and front-end visualization.

I am currently is exploring the multi-omics data analysis in the field of bioinformatics and developing algorithms/tools for better understanding of transcriptome dynamics and clonal enrichment of antigen-receptors in cancers and autoimmune diseases. I am also developing methods for the integration of increasing amount and variety of next-generation sequnecing data for bioinformatics and biologist.
