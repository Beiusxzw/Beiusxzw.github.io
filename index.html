About

I am a PhD student major in bioinformatics in Zhejiang University - University of Edinburgh joint institute. I am currently working in labW led by Dr. Wanlu Liu. I am currently working on genomic algorithm development, multi-omics analysis, and front-end visualization.

I am currently is exploring the multi-omics data analysis in the field of bioinformatics and developing tools for better understanding of epigenetic regulation mechanism in cell fate decision and clonally enrichment of antigen-receptors in cancers and autoimmune diseases. The central topic he focus is the integration of increasing amount and variety of sequnecing data for bioinformatics and biologist. I am also developing algorithms based on deep learning models for analyzing large-scale single-cell multi-omics datasets.
